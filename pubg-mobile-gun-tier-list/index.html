PUBG Mobile: Gun Tier List (Sept 2023)

In this PUBG Weapons Tier List or Best Weapons Guide, we have ranked all the guns in PUBG Mobile from best to worst. The ranking is based on multiple factors.

PlayerUnknown’s Battlegrounds (PUBG) Mobile is a popular battle royale game that has a wide variety of weapons for players to use. While all weapons have their own unique strengths and weaknesses, some are generally considered to be more powerful and effective than others. This has led to the creation of a “gun tier list,” which ranks the various weapons in the game based on their overall performance and usefulness.

It’s important to note that a gun tier list is a subjective ranking system and what works for one player may not work for another. Ultimately, the best weapon for a player will depend on their playstyle, skill level, and the situation they find themselves in.

The highest tier on a PUBG Mobile gun tier list is typically reserved for the most powerful and effective weapons in the game. These include weapons like the AWM, which is a powerful sniper rifle that can take out enemies at long ranges, and the M249, which is a light machine gun with a high rate of fire and a large magazine size.

The middle tiers of a gun tier list are usually reserved for weapons that are solid all-around choices, but may not excel in any particular area. These weapons can still be very useful in the right hands, but may not be as dominant as the top-tier weapons. Examples of mid-tier weapons in PUBG Mobile include the M416 assault rifle and the S686 shotgun.

Finally, the lower tiers of a gun tier list are typically reserved for weapons that are not as effective as their counterparts. These weapons may have low damage output, a slow rate of fire, or other shortcomings that make them less desirable in certain situations. Despite this, they can still be useful in certain scenarios, such as when a player is caught without a better weapon.
