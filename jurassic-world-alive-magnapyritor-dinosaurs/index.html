Jurassic World Alive: Magnapyritor Dinosaurs

June 12, 2018 Gaurav Yadav

Magnapyritor is a God tier dinosaur in Jurassic World Alive. It is a genetically modified hybrid, created by the fusion of Dimetrodon and Pyrritator.

Table of Contents
  • Magnapyritor Hybrid Cost
  • Magnapyritor Stats
  • Magnapyritor Moves
  • Stats per level

Magnapyritor is classified as Unique and costs 500 DNA of Dimetrodon and 50 DNA of Pyrritator, plus 1000 coins to create a random number of DNA strands (anything from 10 to 90). 250 DNA is needed to create this hybrid dino. Both Dimetrodon and Pyrritator should be level 20 in order to fuse and create the hybrid.

Magnapyritor Hybrid Cost

This is an approximation and we are assuming, that on an average 25 DNA is created.

Dimetrodon = 500 * 10 = 5000 DNA

Pyrritator = 50 * 10 = 500 DNA

Coins = 1000 * 10 = 10000 coins
