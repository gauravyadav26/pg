Pokemon Go: Halloween 2019 Event New Research Tasks

October 18, 2019 Gaurav Yadav

Pokemon Go Halloween Event 2019: A Spooky Message is set to begin on October 17, 2019, at 1 PM PDT and lasts until November 1, 2019, at 1 PM PDT.

This event brings a lot of bonuses with it along with one of the most desired:

  • 2x Candy for catching, hatching, or transferring Pokemon!

Halloween Event 2019: A Spooky Message

Halloween 2019 Research Tasks

Update:

  1. Litwick, the Candle Pokémon, can now be encountered in the wild!
  2. Darkrai Raid Boss has a higher difficulty level(22500 HP).

Ghost-type Pokemon will be spawning in significantly increased numbers.

List of Ghost Pokemon with increased spawn:

  • Weedle
  • Kakuna
  • Beedrill
  • Electabuzz
  • Magmar
  • Lapras
  • Mareep
  • Seedot
  • Nuzleaf
  • Sableye
  • Trapinch
  • Cacnea
  • Shuppet
  • Duskull

Shiny Yamask will be available, as well as shiny versions of the Gen 1 starters in costumes! Pikachu is even in on the fun in a Mimikyu outfit.
