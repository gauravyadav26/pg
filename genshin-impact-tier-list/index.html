Genshin Impact Tier List 4.0 (Sept 2023)

Genshin Impact is an open-world action RPG game that has taken the world by storm since its release in September 2020. The game features a vast array of characters, each with their own unique abilities and skills. With so many characters to choose from, it can be overwhelming for players to determine which characters are the best for their playstyle and team composition. In this article, we will be discussing a tier list for Genshin Impact characters.

When it comes to the Genshin Impact tier list, it’s important to note that they are subjective and can vary depending on the player and their specific goals. This tier list is based on the opinions of experienced Genshin Impact players and is intended to provide a general idea of the relative strengths and weaknesses of each character.

S-tier: These characters are the top performers in the game and are essential for any team composition. They have strong abilities, excellent synergy with other characters, and are highly sought after by players.

A-tier: These characters are strong and useful in a variety of situations, but may not be as dominant as S-tier characters. They are a solid choice for any team composition and can help round out a well-balanced team.

B-tier: These characters are decent, but do not particularly stand out. They may have some useful abilities, but may not be as strong as other characters in their element or role. They can still be a valuable addition to a team but may require some careful consideration and strategic planning.

C-tier: They are just not useful in terms of the team game but if a player likes to play them and is an expert user, then go ahead and pick them.
