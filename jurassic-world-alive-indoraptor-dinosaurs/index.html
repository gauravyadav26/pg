Jurassic World Alive: Indoraptor Dinosaurs

June 23, 2018 Gaurav Yadav
Indoraptor is a hybrid dinosaur in Jurassic World Alive, it is a hybrid introduced in Jurassic World: Fallen Kingdom. It is one of the strongest dinosaurs in Jurassic World Alive. It is a genetically modified hybrid, created by the fusion of Indominus Rex, and Velociraptor.

Table of Contents
  • Indoraptor Hybrid Cost
  • Indoraptor Stats
  • Indoraptor Moves
  • Stats per level

It is classified as Unique and costs 50 DNA of Indominus Rex and 2000 DNA of Velociraptor, plus 1000 coins to create a random number of DNA strands (anything from 10 to 90). 100 DNA is needed to create this hybrid dino. Both Indominus Rex and Velociraptor should be level 20 in order to fuse and create the hybrid.
