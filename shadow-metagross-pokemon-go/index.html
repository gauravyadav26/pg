Shadow Metagross: Pokemon Go

Gaurav Yadav

August 8, 2023
Pokemon Go

Shadow Metagross is a Shadow Pokémon that can be obtained through Team GO Rocket battles in the popular mobile game Pokémon GO. It has higher CP, HP, and stronger moves than normal Metagross. However, it also has a lower catch rate and stardust cost to power up.

Shadow Metagross can be purified into a normal Metagross. Shadow Metagross is a psychic and steel-type Pokémon, which makes it weak to fire, ground, and dark-type attacks, but resistant to normal, flying, psychic, and steel-type moves. It is a powerful attacker and a good defender.

Shadow Metagross is considered a top-tier attacker in the Great League, where its CP is capped at 1,500. It has a dual typing of Psychic and Steel, which makes it resistant to Normal, Flying, Psychic, and Steel-type attacks. This makes it a great option against many popular defenders such as Registeel, Jynx, and Alakazam. Its high attack stat and access to powerful STAB moves such as Meteor Mash, Psychic, and Bullet Punch make it a formidable attacker.

In the Ultra and Master League, Shadow Metagross’s CP goes beyond the cap, so it can’t be used in those leagues, but it still can be a great option in the Great League. It’s also a good option in raids, where it can deal significant damage to normal, flying, psychic, and steel-type bosses.
