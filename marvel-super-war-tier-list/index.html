Marvel Super War Tier List (July 2023)

Gaurav Yadav

July 1, 2023
Marvel Super War

Marvel Super War is a mobile game that features a wide array of characters from the Marvel Universe. With so many characters to choose from, it can be overwhelming for players to decide which ones to use in their team. That’s why many players turn to tier lists to get a sense of which characters are the strongest and most effective in the game.

In this article, we’ll look at a tier list for Marvel Super War and provide a brief overview of each character included in the list. Remember that tier lists can be subjective and may not necessarily reflect the strengths and weaknesses of every player’s personal style.

This MSW tier list has been updated to include Gamora released in July 2023

Table of Contents

  • S+ Tier
  • S Tier
  • A Tier

A tier list is a ranking system that categorizes characters based on their power and usefulness in the game. It helps players make informed decisions about which heroes to use in their team and how to build the best team composition.

Please note that the use of any hero depends on your playstyle and how well you know their skills. So you might find some heroes should be in a better tier or lower tier and that’s fine.

Marvel Super War Tier List

The S+Tier heroes are the absolute best in the game. They have strong abilities, good mobility, and excellent damage output. These heroes can easily carry a team to victory and are a must-have in any team composition.

S tier – Top heroes able to carry games that are often picked.

A tier – They are good and in the right hands can easily win you a match, but not as fast as S or S+.
