Jurassic World Alive: Dinosaurs Collection List(1.9)

Gaurav Yadav

AR Games, Jurassic World Alive

Jurassic World Alive has a collection of 176 dinosaurs in version 1.9, which are divided into 5 tiers based on the rarity of spawn.

  1. Common
  2. Rare
  3. Epic
  4. Legendary
  5. Unique

Players can encounter them in the wild or can make hybrids by fusing the DNA of different dinosaurs.

Note: Health and Damage are at level 26
