Marvel Super War: Angel Hero Guide

January 10, 2020 Gaurav Yadav

Angel in Marvel Super War is a Marksman with Normal difficulty and an S Tier Hero. Angel has high burst damage and mobility, so he can break enemy lines and can be a decent tank killer.

Skill Details

Flechettes

Passive: Angel’s every 5 basic attack launches another two feathers for 30% extra basic attack damage.

Judgement

Passive: Enemies struck by Angel’s basic attacks or Sacred Feathers will be marked with one Original Sin. All marks will be expunged from the target by one basic attack from Angel after 4 marks have been accumulated, dealing 10 (+90%Physical Attack) physical damage.

Active: Angel enhances his own attack speed by 20% for 4 seconds. Judgement metamorphosis: A percentage of HP already lost by the target will be converted into true damage when Original Sin is triggered.

Sacred Feathers

Angel releases five feathered assaults on nearby enemies, each dealing 80 (+45%Bonus Physical Attack) physical damage but this is weakened to 25% when striking the same target more than once. These feathered attacks can inflict up to one Original Sin per enemy.

Sacred Feathers metamorphosis: The number of feathers is increased to nine, and if three or more feathers strike the same target, then Angel can inflict one extra Original Sin. Hitting the enemy with basic attacks will help to reduce this ability’s cooldown.

Passive: Angel can select a further ability to undergo metamorphosis every time Angel’s Coming is leveled up. Active: Angel takes flight and heads squarely for the specified enemy hero, dealing 130 (+50%Bonus Physical Attack) physical damage and a 50% slow for 2.5 seconds. Early Game Guide

Angel is weak in early game as other marksman will be able to outrange him at this point so be carefull and use support.

End Game Guide

After level 6 angel will be able to outrange other marksman but player need to wait for the right moment to dive in and deal high damage. He is prone to crowd control.
