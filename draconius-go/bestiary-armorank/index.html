SUMMARY

Armorank is a Water type Draconius GO creature with 4000 Max CP. Armorank best moveset is Splash with Power of Ice. Armorank takes increased damage from Air and reduced damage from Earth type attacks.

~ LORE ~ARMORANK

Armorank’s unassuming sway and two buckteeth belie his astounding power and resilience. This armored monster has ferocious attacks and extraordinary defenses, qualities unmatched by many of the most powerful creatures.

slumbervision

While Armorank’s body is sturdy, its home is a peace of delicate architecture. That’s why this creature protects its home fiercely from any intruder.

iluminiele

Armorank use their gigantic inscisors and massive tail flippers to ravage plains and forests. Only the most skilled warriors and mages can successfully capture an armorank.
