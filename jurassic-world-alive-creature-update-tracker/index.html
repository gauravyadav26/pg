Jurassic World Alive CREATURE UPDATE Tracker

Gaurav Yadav

October 13, 2023
Jurassic World Alive

Jurassic World Alive is a popular augmented-reality mobile game that has captured the hearts of dinosaur lovers everywhere. The game allows players to collect and battle virtual dinosaurs and has become a must-have for fans of the Jurassic Park franchise.

One of the game’s most exciting aspects is the constant stream of new creatures being added to the roster and the constant changes made to them. Here, we’ll track all the creature updates for Jurassic World Alive.

  • Decelerating Counter added

Resistances:
