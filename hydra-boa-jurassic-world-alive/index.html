Hydra Boa: Jurassic World Alive

June 25, 2022 Gaurav Yadav

This gargantuan boa is named after the many-headed serpent slain by Heracles in Greek myth. InGen scientists chose the name due to a special characteristic of the massive snake’s scales. Unlike those of other species, the scales of the Hydra Boa’s head and neck grow thicker and brighter each time the snake molts, as if the creature were growing a new head each time!

Hydra Boa belongs to the high Apex Tier.
