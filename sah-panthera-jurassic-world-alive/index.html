Sah Panthera: Jurassic World Alive

Sah Panthera in Jurassic World Alive is of Apex rarity and a Wild Card creature.

Sah’ is the Tibetan word for the modern snow leopard. However, Sah Panthera more resembles the mythical Snow Lion of Buddhist tradition.

Sah Panthera belongs to the Tyrant Tier.
