Team Go Rocket: Grunts Dialogues and Counters (August 2021)

September 1, 2021 Gaurav Yadav
Team Go Rocket Grunts battles can be quite difficult and tricky, so you need to have a team that has good counters to them. You can know what kind of Pokemon the Grunt will have from their dialogue.

Team Go Rocket randomly and independently selects one Pokemon from each of the lists below to build a team, which are sent in the same order.

Table of Contents
  • Team Go Rocket Dialogues
  • Grunts Counters
  • Team Go Rocket Leader Counters

Pokemon in Green Box has a chance of being captured after the battle.

Addition Info:

  • Grunts never use their Protect Sheild but leaders will always try to use their Protect Shields.
  • Pokemon dropped by leaders can be shiny.

The Team Go Rocket grunts dialogue indicates the strongest Pokemon, not the one you can catch.

As of August 2021, there are 20 different Grunts that you can encounter.

Team Go Rocket Grunts Dialogues

Are you scared of psychics that use unseen power?

Grunts who taunt you with “Are you scared of psychics that use unseen power?” dialogue will use Psychic-type Pokemon. So it’s best to counter by using strong Ghost, Dark, and Bug-types.

Possible Encounters:

Possible counters:

  • Tyranitar
  • Weavile
  • Houndoom
  • Haunter

Battle against my Flying-type Pokemon!

Grunts who taunt you with “Battle against my Flying-type Pokemon!?” dialogue will use Flying-type Pokemon. So it’s best to counter by using strong Rock, Electric, or Ice-types.
