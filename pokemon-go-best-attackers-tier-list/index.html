Pokemon Go Best Attackers Tier List (Sept 2023)

The best Pokemon in Pokemon Go will mostly depend on how you’re to use them. In this Pokemon Go tier list, we will rank Pokemon based on their strengths as attackers. Pokemon Go is a popular mobile game that allows players to catch and train virtual creatures known as Pokemon. In the game, players can battle their Pokemon against other players’ Pokemon in gyms.

One important factor in winning these battles is the moveset of the Pokemon, as different moves have different attack and defense statistics. In this article, we will discuss the best attackers in Pokemon Go, according to the tier list.

This Pokemon Go tier list has been updated till Sept 2023.
