OPR tool for new portal launched in Brazil and Korea!

April 23, 2017 Gaurav Yadav

Operation Portal Recon (OPR) is an Ingress tool used to submit new portal request for Ingress players. These Submissions are used to create new portals in Ingress and new PokeStops or PokeGyms in Pokemon GO.

Portal are points of interest or action points in Ingress, just like PokeStops and PokeGyms are in Pokemon GO.

Niantic had closed portal submission some time back but now it has announced that their Operation Portal Recon (OPR) tool for Ingress has launched in Brazil and Korea.



Official Communication:

Agents,

Starting today, we will be opening Portal submission requests via the Ingress scanner for Level 8+ Agents in Brazil and Korea. Before submitting potential Portal candidates, please ensure that you familiarize yourself with the guidelines for high-quality Portals. Portal submissions will be analyzed in the Operation Portal Recon tool by our Beta-tester Ingress Agents. While not all Portal submissions will be accepted, your contributions will enable us to expand the Portal network in the future. As always, we appreciate the diligence and help provided by our Agents to help curate and expand the Portal Network.

For full details regarding high-quality Portal candidates, please review the following guidelines: https://support.ingress.com/hc/en-us/articles/207343987-Candidate-Portal-criteria

We previously shared the news about PokeStops images and information been updated and addition of new PokeStops which was confirmed by Niantic. (approx 11000 edits and 9000 additions)

As we know data for Pokemon GO is based on Ingress portals, so with updates in Ingress Pokemon GO map data will also get updated.
