Andrewtops: Jurassic World Alive

Andrewtops has a unique skill among the creatures of Jurassic World. Like the modern Argentinian Tegu, this hybrid can raise its own body temperature, becoming a temporarily warm-blooded reptile! DPG specialists believe this capability may come from its mammalian ancestry.
